Better Learn is a companion extension for Up Learn.

The Skip video feature is available as a bookmarklet for mobile devices.

To save the bookmarklet, hold the Better Learn Bookmarklet button and press Bookmark Link or similar to keep it in your browser's bookmarks.

To use it, click on the bookmark from your bookmarks menu when on an Up Learn video lesson to skip the content of the current video and only display questions.
